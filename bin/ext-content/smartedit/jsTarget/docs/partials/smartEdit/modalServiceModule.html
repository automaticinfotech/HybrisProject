The modalServiceModule

The modal service module is a module devoted to providing an easy way to create, use, manage, and style modal windows, and it contains all the components related to achieving this goal.

Use the modalService to open modal windows.

Once a modal window is opened, you can use it's ModalManager to manage the modal, such as closing the modal, adding buttons, or chaning the title.

Additionally you may use button actions or button styles to affect how buttons look and behave on the modal window.
